GoSafeMed | Your Partner in Medical & Rescue Preparedness

GoSafeMed is a specialized supply chain integrator for Emergency Medical Devices and Rescue Equipment. We help partners across the EMS (Emergency Medical Services), Occupational Health & Safety (OHS), and Outdoor Recreation sectors access high-standard safety solutions.

We focus on delivering essential rescue hardware, specifically Spinal Boards and Foldable/Simple Stretchers, alongside our signature First Aid Kits. Whether you are equipping a high-risk industrial site, a modern corporate office, or preparing for hiking and camping adventures, our two core lines—the Classic Collection and the Elite-Guard Series—ensure you have the right gear for the right environment.

How We Help You:

  • Simplifying Your Sourcing: We help you eliminate the headache of fragmented procurement by consolidating everything from personal IFAKs to professional Spinal Boards and medical consumables into one reliable, one-stop supply chain.
  • Ensuring Global Compliance: We assist your market entry and reduce legal risks by providing equipment that strictly meets CE, ISO 13485, and FDA standards, backed by the expert technical data you need for seamless safety audits.
  • Optimizing Emergency Outcomes: We empower your team and clients to improve survival rates with lightweight, modular rescue gear designed for rapid access in both professional tactical scenarios and recreational outdoor settings.
  • Reducing Financial & Inventory Strain: We support your business growth with project-based flexibility and tailored inventory solutions, helping you fulfill large contracts or retail demands without the burden of heavy capital investment.
