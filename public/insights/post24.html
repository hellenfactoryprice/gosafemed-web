Beyond Tents: How Outdoor Brands Increase Average Order Value with Just Three Safety Kits

JUL 09, 2026

For most outdoor brands, adding a new product category can feel risky.

More SKUs mean more inventory, more training, and more complexity.

So the question becomes:

How can you increase average order value without making your business more complicated?

Many outdoor brands are finding the answer in one surprisingly simple category: first aid kits.

Not because they want to become medical suppliers.

But because safety naturally belongs alongside every outdoor adventure.

One Product Category. Three Customer Needs.

Rather than offering dozens of different kits, many brands keep it simple.

MINI

Perfect for customers buying hiking gear, picnic equipment, or day-trip essentials.

A compact kit that's easy to add at checkout.

STANDARD

Designed for family camping, caravans, and weekend adventures.

Often purchased together with rooftop tents, camping furniture, and cooking equipment.

PLUS

For overlanding, remote travel, and longer expeditions where professional-grade preparation matters.

This premium option complements higher-value outdoor equipment and appeals to experienced adventurers.

Easier for Customers. Easier for Sales Teams.

A three-level range is easy for retail staff to explain.

Instead of asking customers to compare dozens of products, the conversation becomes simple:

“Where are you going?”

The answer naturally leads to MINI, STANDARD, or PLUS.

More Than Another Product

For outdoor brands, first aid kits aren't just another SKU. They're an opportunity to:

  • Increase average order value
  • Complete the outdoor experience
  • Offer customers one more essential item before they leave

Sometimes, growing your business isn't about adding more products.

It's about adding the right ones.

Looking to Expand Your Outdoor Range?

GoSafeMed helps outdoor brands build practical safety collections with a simple three-level system:

  • MINI – Everyday Adventures
  • STANDARD – Family Camping
  • PLUS – Remote Expeditions

Designed to integrate naturally into existing outdoor product ranges without creating unnecessary complexity.
